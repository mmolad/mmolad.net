Secure Your Gear with an Authenticator.

The Battle.net® Mobile Authenticator is a free* application that provides a unique, single-use code to log in to Battle.net. Once you've downloaded and linked your Mobile Authenticator, you start up the app, request a code, and log in. It's that simple.

Whether safeguarding your Diablo® III legendary loot, your StarCraft® II ladder ranking, or your World of Warcraft® characters, the Battle.net Mobile Authenticator serves as an additional layer of security for your account.

*Available for iPhone®, iPod® touch, Android� phone, BlackBerry®, or Windows® Phone 7 devices.

I need help with Recruit A Friend.

Recruit A Friend is a referral system that allows you to invite friends to play World of Warcraft and receive exclusive in-game benefits when you play together.

If you recruited a friend in the last 30 days but things aren't working as expected, try re-sending the invitation. This resolves most Recruit A Friend issues.

Check out the links to the right to learn more about how to recruit a friend, claiming rewards, and other helpful tips.

I have feedback on the game or service.

While we value the feedback and perspective our players offer, Blizzard Customer Support cannot accept suggestions directly. Suggestions must follow one of two avenues:

Forums

Suggestions can be submitted to the development teams through our official forums. The forums provide a valuable opportunity for us to collect feedback from those who know our games best--our players.

When you create a suggestion post on the forums, select the forum that most closely matches the subject of your feedback.

  • World of Warcraft forums
  • StarCraft II forums
  • Diablo III forums

Note: You must have a StarCraft II license or a World of Warcraft license to post on their respective forums. If you have a StarCraft II, World of Warcraft, or Diablo III license, you can post on the Diablo III Forums.

In-game (World of Warcraft only)

Suggestions submitted in-game will go directly to our development team; Customer Support will not provide a response. To submit a suggestion in-game:

  1. Press the ESC key to bring up the Game Menu, then click Help.
  2. Click Submit Suggestion.
  3. Enter your suggestion. Be sure to follow the guidelines on the submission form.
  4. Click Submit.

Account compromised? We can help.

If someone else gained access to your Battle.net account, don't panic. Support is available 24/7 to get you back in game and investigate your missing goods. To begin, follow the steps below:

Change Your Password

1

If you can still log in to your Battle.net account, change your password. If your personal information has changed, this could be a sign of an account compromise.

  • Password Reset

Secure Your Computer

2

It's easy to secure your account if you know how. Visit our Security Checklist to make the most of your account's security. Review our Security Software page to further improve your system's defenses.

  • Security Checklist
  • Security Software
  • Types of Account Thefts
  • The Impact of Buying Gold

Request an Investigation

3

  • If you can't log in to Battle.net and you are missing characters, heroes, or items in World of Warcraft or Diablo III, use our Account Recovery web form.
  • If you can log in to Battle.net but you are missing items or heroes in Diablo III, request a rollback.
  • If you can log in to Battle.net but you are missing items or characters in World of Warcraft, submit a ticket.

  • Account Recovery web form
  • Diablo III Account Rollback
  • World of Warcraft Account Compromise
  • Diablo III Account Compromise

Protect Your Account

4

The Battle.net Authenticator and Battle.net Mobile Authenticator app are easy ways to add an additional layer of security to your account. They provide a unique authentication code that you enter at login. Learn more on our Battle.net Authenticator article.

  • Add an Authenticator
  • Battle.net Authenticator Basics
